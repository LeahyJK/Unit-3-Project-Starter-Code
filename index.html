A Stroll

While driving down a street, you see a small path through the treeline to your left. You hop out of your car and stand at the line. Do you go into the woods, or back to your car?

You get in your car and get driving again. You make your way home, ready to see your mom for the first time in years.

You head into the woods. You feel exhilarated at the potential, and what you might find. After a moment, you see a small path leading out of the treeline

While driving, you get stuck in traffic. You're sitting behind a stoplight for hours before you mom calls you complaining about you never coming over

The End

You follow the path until you find a small dilapidated cottage. Looking around, you notice that it's actually only a short walk down the hill from your mom's house. Pleased that you found a shortcut, you head up the hill and meet up with you mom for the first time in a while.

The End
